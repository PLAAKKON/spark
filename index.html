The EV Charging Challenge

We at Spark find that the greatest problem within EV charging is specifically in Europe's largest cities: London, Paris, Berlin. These metropolitan areas face unique challenges with limited space, high demand, and infrastructure constraints that traditional charging solutions cannot adequately address.

London

8.9 million residents, limited street parking, urgent need for smart EV infrastructure

Paris

2.2 million residents, historic districts with space constraints, growing EV adoption

Berlin

3.7 million residents, ambitious climate goals, need for efficient charging networks

About Spark Robot

Spark Robot is at the forefront of revolutionizing electric vehicle charging infrastructure through advanced AI robotics and smart technology solutions.
